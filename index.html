Meet Our Coaches

Director

Nikki Roth

Nikki Roth is a world-class tennis and pickleball coach with certifications from USPTA, PTR, USTA, and the USA Pickleball Association. With years of experience, she brings a wealth of knowledge to the court and ensures every player achieves their full potential.

Assistant Coach

Moises Goncalves

Moises brings 16 years of racquet experience and has represented Venezuela in international tennis tournaments, including South American Championships. His dedication to technique and passion for the game are inspiring to all of his students.
